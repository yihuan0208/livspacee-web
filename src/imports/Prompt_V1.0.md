\f0\fs24 \cf0 Design a premium international B2B corporate website for a company specializing in custom furniture solutions for mid-scale hotels and serviced apartments.\
\
The company should NOT be presented as a furniture factory, furniture retailer, or product catalog. Instead, position it as a trusted Hospitality Furniture Project Partner providing integrated Design-to-Delivery solutions, including furniture design collaboration, engineering support, manufacturing, quality control, packaging, and project delivery.\
\
The overall visual style should be modern, architectural, minimal, elegant, and highly professional. Draw inspiration from premium architecture firms, hospitality consultants, and high-end manufacturing companies rather than consumer furniture brands. Use generous white space, large authentic project photography, refined typography, subtle animations, and a clean grid system.\
\
The website should communicate four key messages:\
1. We understand hospitality projects.\
2. We deliver reliably and efficiently.\
3. We have proven project experience.\
4. We are easy to work with.\
\
Avoid an e-commerce appearance. Avoid product-heavy layouts, SKU grids, promotional banners, flashy effects, excessive gradients, or retail-oriented visuals.\
\
Use real hospitality spaces instead of isolated furniture whenever possible.\
\
## Website Structure\
\
### 1. Hero Section\
A strong headline introducing the company as a hospitality furniture project partner.\
A high-quality hotel or serviced apartment project image.\
Primary CTA: Request a Quote.\
Secondary CTA: View Projects.\
\
### 2. Why Choose Us\
Present four core advantages using clean cards:\
- Fast & Predictable Delivery\
- Hospitality Standards Compliance\
- Easy Maintenance & Long-Term Operation\
- Professional Design Collaboration\
\
### 3. Company Highlights\
Display key business statistics such as years of experience, completed projects, countries served, and repeat clients.\
\
### 4. Featured Projects\
Show two hotel projects and two serviced apartment projects.\
Each project should include a large image, project type, location, and a short description.\
Allow visitors to explore more project details.\
\
### 5. Project Delivery Process\
Illustrate a standardized Design-to-Delivery workflow based on a clear project timeline, from design coordination and shop drawings to manufacturing, quality control, packaging, shipping, and delivery.\
\
### 6. Manufacturing Capability\
Introduce the factory, production workflow, materials, quality control, packaging standards, and production capacity.\
Focus on reliability, consistency, and execution rather than factory size.\
\
### 7. Contact\
A clean contact section including inquiry form, email, WhatsApp, and clear call-to-action encouraging visitors to discuss their projects.\
\
## Design Guidelines\
\
- Premium B2B corporate style\
- Architecture-inspired aesthetics\
- Hospitality-focused imagery\
- Large authentic photography\
- Minimal color palette\
- Spacious layouts\
- Strong visual hierarchy\
- Elegant typography\
- Desktop-first responsive design\
- Every section should naturally lead users toward making an inquiry\
\
The website should make visitors feel that they are partnering with an experienced hospitality project specialist rather than simply purchasing furniture.}
